Devspark is an exceptional hackathon that merges technology, innovation, and wildlife conservation. It challenges participants to devise solutions for the conservation of endangered species and their habitats. Going beyond wildlife, Devspark encourages participants to tackle real-world problems across various industries. From sustainable agriculture to healthcare and climate change, participants are empowered to develop transformative solutions. Through collaboration and creativity, Devspark inspires participants to make a lasting impact. By fostering a unique blend of technological expertise and environmental consciousness, this hackathon provides a platform for participants to address pressing challenges and create innovative solutions with far-reaching implications.

What is
DEV SPARK

Devspark gives people a chance to be creative, so they may come up with new and exciting technology solutions for the world. It's a great way to meet other professionals and learn about recent advances in your field while also having fun and discovering new prospects. Devspark is committed to make a difference by focusing on sustainable development that takes into account environmental, social, and economic issues. Participants can become a part of future-shaping, sustainably-focused projects in this incredible event.

RULES

1) Each team should have a minimum of one and a maximum of four members.

2) All VIT students, regardless of their year, branch, or any other criterion, are welcome to participate in this hackathon.

3) They are free to employ any technology they choose. For instance they can use any web development , machine learning and blockchain technologies.

4) Refrain from copying code from AI tools.Your implementations will be carefully examined, and any code duplication is strongly discouraged.

5) Teams must complete their project preparation, presentation practice, and idea execution within the time frame specified in the schedule.There won't be any added time.

FAQS

1) What will be a part of DevSpark?
-> DevSpark is an all-inclusive event containing Workshops, mentoring sessions, coding competitions and a Hackathon which is is being organised by IEEE-IAS and IEEE-WIE.

2) What is the theme of DevSpark?
-> Yes, DevSpark is a themed Hack with the theme being Wildlife.

3) Where do I register for the event?
-> The registration of this event will be done through VTOP.
VTOP->SW Events->Event Registration->Event Title(YANTRA-23:DEVSPARK)

4) Is DevSpark a team Hackathon?
-> Yes, DevSpark is a team Hackathon. A team comprises of 2-4 individuals.

5) How many rounds will be there?
-> There will be 2 rounds, the first one being a ‘review-0’ and then after that there will be an implementation round.
